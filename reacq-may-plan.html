May Plan · 3 of 5 · Convert Users

Convert — TVOD + Freemium

Three product mechanics, all converging on dual-CTA: ₹20 TVOD or ₹199 full.

TVOD pitch flow Movie pass

Triggered when user lands on a movie detail page from Ad/CLM.

  1. User lands on movie detail page from CLM/Ad click
  2. 2-min teaser auto-plays (free) — emotional hook lands
  3. At hook moment, overlay freezes video: "Continue watching this movie?"
  4. Side-by-side CTAs: ₹20 just this · ₹199 unlimited
  5. If neither → fallback "Watch 5 min more free" → coin gate at end
Freemium / Free zone Seed engagement

Triggered for users who reject paywall on first session.

  1. Open app → Free zone with curated lighter content
  2. User watches X mins / Y episodes for free
  3. At climax / cliffhanger → pitch overlay (don't interrupt mid-scene)
  4. Same dual-CTA: ₹20 unlock this · ₹199 unlock everything
  5. If user dismisses → unlock 5 more free min, repeat at next climax
Comparison framing — every paywall, side-by-side

Replace single-CTA paywall with dual-option visual. Don't force commitment to subscription.

Why this works: users who reject ₹199 (commitment shock) often accept ₹20. TVOD purchase = paid customer = remarketing list for subscription upsell later.
