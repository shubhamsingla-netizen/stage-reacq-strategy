Walkthrough · 7 of 10 · Seg B

Seg B · Improve Conversions

Product experiments targeting the drop-off patterns

🧱 Tiered Sub for In-Trial Revokers In dev

Users who cancel mandate during trial (still in ₹1 window) will see a tiered pitch — ₹99 / ₹399 quarterly / ₹699 yearly — instead of just the default ₹199.

Goal: convert revokers before they fully churn, using the same tiered insight that worked for re-acq.

▶️ Continue Watching for Partial Watchers In dev

Users who started but didn't finish content → get a targeted "resume where you left off" flow when they return.

Content-pull is stronger than promo-pull. C1 cohort (unfinished watchers) already has 35% D0 return rate — this codifies it.

Both ship this month

Tiered-sub for in-trial revokers + Continue Watching are the two highest-leverage Seg B plays currently in eng.

Walkthrough · 8 of 10 · New Paywall

Free till hook → 3-option decoy

A new paywall concept for re-activation cohorts

The idea

Returning user lands on free content until the hook moment (first cliffhanger / high-retention point) — then sees a 3-option paywall structured as a decoy.

Pay-per-view
₹20
TVOD — this movie only
Weekly Decoy
₹49
Full access · 7 days
Subscription
₹99
Full access · 1 month

Why this works

TVOD ₹20 lowers the commitment floor. Weekly ₹49 anchors the user on "weekly cost" and makes Monthly ₹99 the obviously-smart choice — same decoy mechanic that drove 2× revenue per user in re-acq tiered pricing.

Ships to Seg A & Seg B re-entry flows. Replaces the default ₹1 trial or ₹199 hard sub at the critical return moment.
